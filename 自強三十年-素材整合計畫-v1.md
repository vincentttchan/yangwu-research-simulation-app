## 1. 圓明園銅版畫（雙層方案 · 背景）

### 1.1 位置與規格

| 屬性 | 值 |
|---|---|
| 路徑 | `assets/sketches/yuanmingyuan-engraving.webp` |
| 位置 | 引言頁全屏背景，固定 `position: fixed; inset: 0` |
| 尺寸 | 寬度涵蓋 100vw、`object-fit: cover` |
| 不透明度 | **8–10%**（最後微調以引文仍清楚為準） |
| 動畫 | `ambient-drift` 60 秒一週期，scale 1.04 ↔ 1.06 + 位移 ±0.5% |
| 層級 | `z-index: 0`（最底層） |

### 1.2 取材建議

**首選**：海晏堂西面 銅版畫（你上傳的那張）
- 構圖完整、有 12 獸首坐姿、有兩翼建築
- 直接是「**兔首的原始場景**」，與角落兔首形成宏觀-微觀對話

**次選**：大水法（Da Shui Fa）銅版畫
- 西洋樓系列最廣為人知的一張
- 拱門、噴泉、瓶座完整

### 1.3 後製處理（**重要**）

無論你選用哪張原圖，整合前需做：

1. **裁切右上角文字**（如「海晏堂西面 十」），或用 CSS mask 蓋住
2. **去飽和**：純單色處理，色階向 `#304143`（Deep Slate）偏移
3. **降對比**：原圖中段對比度太強，建議**整體加亮 20%**讓畫面更柔
4. **儲存為 webp**，目標 < 120KB（背景大圖可放寬到 200KB）

### 1.4 AI 重生（若用 AI 處理現有圖）

如果你想用 AI 把現有銅版畫改成更貼合風格，prompt：

```
Convert this image to a single-tone sepia/ink wash on
aged cream paper background (#E5E0D9). Apply heavy
fading, reduce contrast by 30%, soften all lines.
Remove all text and stamps. Background should be uniform
pale cream. The result should feel like a 19th-century
print reproduction in a museum book.

Preserve: all architectural detail, the 12 zodiac figures
on plinths, the central fountain, both wing buildings.

Do NOT: add color, add new elements, change architecture,
add any text or signature.
```

### 2.2 AI 生圖指示詞

```
A single ink-line drawing of the Bronze Rabbit Head (兔首)
from the Haiyantang Zodiac Fountain of Yuanmingyuan, in
damaged/weathered state — as it appeared after the 1860
looting, surface showing patina, one ear partially chipped,
expression contemplative and mournful.

Aesthetic: traditional Chinese 白描 (baimiao) technique —
pure line work without shading or fill. Brush-and-ink
quality with controlled variation in line weight (細若遊
絲 to 粗如鐵骨). NOT Western engraving, NOT hatching.

Composition: 3/4 profile facing gently leftward, plinth
fragment visible at bottom. Negative space generous above
and right of the head.

Reference style: 工筆白描 of late Qing court paintings,
郎世寧's animal studies, 《十二月令圖》line quality,
點石齋畫報 brushwork. Lines should feel HAND-DRAWN with
bamboo brush on rice paper — organic, not mechanical.

Single ink tone (Deep Slate #304143) on transparent or
cream (#E5E0D9) background. Aspect 4:5 portrait. No text,
no labels, no color, no shading fills.
```

## 3. 中央之國 Aerial Map（地圖頁用，非引言頁）

### 3.1 位置與規格

| 屬性 | 值 |
|---|---|
| 路徑 | `assets/map/east-asia-sinocentric.webp` 或 `.svg` |
| 位置 | **地圖頁**全屏底圖（非引言頁） |
| 尺寸 | 1600×1000 寬高比 |
| 不透明度 | 8–15%（依年份不同調整） |
| 動畫 | 國家比例隨年份動態縮放 |
| 層級 | 地圖頁的最底層 |

### 3.2 比例隨年份變化機制

| 年份 | 中國視覺寬度 | 日本視覺寬度 | 隱喻 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1860 | **60%** | 8% | 中央之國、邊陲島嶼 |
| 1872 | 55% | 12% | 明治維新已 5 年 |
| 1885 | 50% | 15% | 中法戰後日本崛起 |
| 1894 春 | 45% | 20% | 戰前夕 |
| 1894 秋 | 40% | 25% | 黃海大戰 |
| **1895** | **35%** | **35%** | **馬關 — 平起平坐** |

技術：SVG 各國 `<g>` 包裝、`transform: scale(...)` 年份驅動。**比例變化不可逆**，玩家走過就回不去。

### 3.3 AI 生圖指示詞（1860 baseline 版）

```
A Qing-dynasty-style atlas map of East Asia, drawn in the
SINICENTRIC tradition: China visually DOMINANT, filling
~60% of the canvas width. Surrounding peripheral territories
deliberately drawn SMALLER than actual geographic scale,
pushed toward the edges.

Aesthetic: 皇輿全覽圖 (Kangxi Atlas, 1718) × 大清萬年一統
地理全圖 (1810). Brush-and-ink coastlines with gentle wave
patterns in seas. Mountains as small silhouetted peaks (NOT
Western hachures). Latitude/longitude grid as faint lines.

CHINA (visual subject):
  Full extent — Manchuria to Hainan. Interior provinces
  lightly silhouetted. Coastal provinces 直隸/山東/江蘇/
  浙江/福建/廣東 emphasized. Max 8 cities marked as small
  circles.

PERIPHERAL (scaled DOWN intentionally):
  • Korean Peninsula:   ~60% true size, pushed NE
  • Japan (本州+九州+四國): ~45% true size, far right
  • Taiwan/Formosa:     ~70% size
  • Hainan:             ~70% size
  Do NOT show: Russia, Vietnam, SE Asia, Philippines.

Labels: ONLY tiny italic Western romanization for 5–7 cities
total — PEKING, SHANGHAI, CANTON, NANKING, FOOCHOW, EDO,
NAGASAKI, SEOUL. NO Chinese characters anywhere.

Decorations:
  • Compass: traditional 八卦-style rose, top-right
  • Scale bar: bottom-left, in 里 (LI)
  • Subtle silk-scroll texture, edges fraying

Color: monochrome sepia/ink on aged cream paper (#E5E0D9).
Heavy fading and patina. Image should feel like a fragile
centuries-old silk scroll fragment.

Aspect 16:10 landscape. Will be displayed at 8-15% opacity.

References: 皇輿全覽圖, 大清萬年一統地理全圖, 海國圖志東亞圖.
AVOID Western nautical charts, Bellin, Admiralty, equal-scale
Mercator projection.
```

### 3.4 替代方案：SVG 自畫

如果 AI 生不出符合的中央之國地圖，**改為手寫 SVG**：
- 我可以基於現有 `aerial-bg.svg` 重做（已是 SVG 結構，方便加 transform）
- 工程約 2 小時
- 優點：完全可控、年份動畫直接 inline 寫
- 缺點：藝術感不如 AI 圖
