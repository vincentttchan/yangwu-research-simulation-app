## 一、風格基準（所有圖片共用）

全系列採用 **19 世紀銅版蝕刻畫 / 鋼版版畫（steel engraving / copper etching）** 風格。

**統一規則：**
- 細密交叉排線（fine crosshatching），手工刻版質感，無攝影感
- 各圖有獨立色調傾向（見各條目），但底層皆為單色版畫結構
- 有輕微做舊質感（aged paper texture）
- 不使用現代濾鏡或 AI 平滑感

**尺寸規格：**
| 類型 | 尺寸 | 備注 |
|---|---|---|
| 城市場景背景 | 1920×1080px | 橫向 16:9 |
| 事件 modal 插圖 | 1440×900px | 橫向 16:10 |
| 格式 | WebP | 壓縮至 300KB 以下 |

**存放路徑：**
```
assets/
  sketches/     ← 人物肖像、敘事插圖（現有）
  city/         ← 城市場景背景（需新建）
  events/       ← 事件 modal 插圖（需新建）
```

### C-01 `city-shanghai.webp` 🔴 優先最高
- **存放**：`assets/city/`
- **色調**：暖琥珀 / 棕褐（warm sepia-amber）
- **歷史特質**：東西融合，繁忙貿易港，外灘洋行，江南製造局
- **對應熱點（座標為百分比，左上為原點）**：
  - `碼頭工人` (18%, 78%) → 左側前景，地面層，苦力挑貨
  - `外灘洋行` (45%, 38%) → 畫面正中，中高度，英美法三國旗，買辦
  - `中式帆船` (45%, 58%) → 正中水面，三桅篷船
  - `西洋輪船` (68%, 55%) → 右中水面，單煙囪明輪船
  - `江南製造煙囪` (72%, 32%) → **右側偏高**，陸地上紅磚煙囪，高於所有建築
- **修訂說明**：上一版缺少江南製造煙囪（最重要的陸上地標），且洋行旗幟應為英美法三面而非僅英旗。構圖必須在右側背景可見高聳紅磚煙囪，否則玩家無法找到解鎖「寫信予奕訢」的線索熱點。

**AI 提示詞：**
```
19th century copper etching, panoramic view of Shanghai Bund and surroundings, 1865. The composition is divided into five visual zones that must each be clearly readable:

LEFT FOREGROUND (0–25% width, lower half): A stone embankment at dock level. Four or five Chinese coolies in conical hats carry heavy canvas bales on bamboo shoulder poles, their faces expressionless. Coiled ropes and wooden crates on the ground.

CENTER (35–55% width, mid-height): The Bund waterfront — a row of two-storey colonial counting houses with arched windows and iron railings. Three national flags fly prominently from flagpoles above the roofline: the British Union Jack, the American Stars and Stripes, and the French Tricolour. In front of the entrance, a Chinese comprador in a Western-style frock coat and queue hairstyle speaks with a foreign merchant — a new class of intermediary.

CENTER WATER (40–65% width, lower half): The Huangpu River. A Chinese three-masted junk with bamboo mat sails sits heavy in the water at center. Slightly to the right, a single-funnel British paddle steamer moves toward the dock, its smokestack releasing a column of black smoke.

RIGHT BACKGROUND — CRITICAL (65–80% width, upper third): Rising well above the roofline of all waterfront buildings, two tall red-brick industrial smokestacks of the Jiangnan Arsenal emit heavy dark smoke. These must be the tallest visible structures in the right portion of the image, unmistakably industrial and Chinese-made yet Western in technique.

OVERALL: Warm sepia-amber tones. Dense fine crosshatching. The image should feel cosmopolitan and transitional — ancient trade patterns and new industrial power coexisting uneasily on the same waterfront. Horizontal 16:9.
```

### C-02 `city-beijing.webp` 🟡
- **存放**：`assets/city/`
- **色調**：冷石灰 + 暗金（cool imperial stone-grey with muted gold on rooftiles）
- **歷史特質**：天朝中樞，總理衙門與同文館在此誕生，守舊官員與改革派的張力場所
- **對應鐵釘**：1861 總理衙門（第一外交機構）、1862 同文館（第一外語學校，倭仁反對）

**AI 提示詞：**
```
19th century steel engraving, Beijing near the Imperial City, 1861. Two visual zones in tension within one composition. Left two-thirds: the monumental old order — towering grey brick walls of the Forbidden City, Qing officials in embroidered mandarin robes conferring in clusters, sedan chairs, guards in traditional armor. Right one-third: the intrusion of the new — a modest two-storey building with a Western-style arched doorway and a small painted signboard in both Chinese characters and European letters, suggesting a foreign language school or diplomatic office (Tongwen Guan / Zongli Yamen). Two Western diplomats in dark frock coats and top hats stand near this doorway, observed with cold suspicion by a senior Qing official. The composition feels like two worlds colliding at one edge. Cool stone-grey tones throughout. Subtle muted gold on Forbidden City rooftiles visible in the distance. Fine crosshatching. Horizontal 16:9.
```
